Welcome to chefcherrykitchen

Chef'de cuisine catering services

Chef'de cuisine catering services, is one of the few indigenous catering services in Nigeria that is built on Innovation. It was founded by Chef Cherry and Chef Chocolate both of whom graduated from Yekods culinary school situated in the northern part of Nigeria with a diploma certificate in combined courses (Africana dishes, Intercontinental dishes, and Pastries) out of their collective passion to make quality and affordable catering services available.
